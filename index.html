Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? I've got answers. Here are the most common questions I receive from potential clients.

How do you determine project pricing?

+

I provide custom quotes based on project complexity, timeline, and specific requirements. Most projects are priced per-project rather than hourly, ensuring you know exactly what you'll pay upfront. I always provide a detailed estimate after our initial consultation, which is completely free.

What's included in your project delivery?

+

Every project includes source files, documentation, 30 days of free support, and revisions as outlined in our agreement. For websites, you also get SEO setup, mobile optimization, and security features. I believe in delivering complete solutions, not half-finished work.

How quickly can you deliver my project?

+

Most websites are delivered within 2-3 weeks, data analysis projects in 1-2 weeks, and branding projects in 1 week. I provide realistic timelines during consultation and keep you updated throughout. Rush delivery is available for an additional 25% fee if needed.

Do you offer ongoing support after project completion?

+

Yes! All projects include 30 days of free support for bug fixes and minor adjustments. After that, I offer maintenance packages or hourly support at €25/hour for updates, improvements, and ongoing assistance. Many clients choose to work with me long-term.

What makes you different from other freelancers?

+

I focus on results, not just deliverables. My websites don't just look good—they convert visitors into customers and rank on Google. I provide direct, personal service with guaranteed delivery dates, and I'm always available to discuss your project. Plus, I offer enterprise-level solutions at freelancer prices.
